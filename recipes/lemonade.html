Vintage Lemonade Recipe

Description

Making lemonade the old-fashioned way is a breeze, but if you’re brand new to the Kitchen, it could feel scary. so follow along.

This easy recipe only requires three ingredients — lemons, sugar, and water. Juice the lemons, remove the seeds, and add the juice to a pitcher, with or without the pulp. Next, add the sugar, sweetening to taste. Finally, fill the pitcher with water, leaving plenty of room for cracked ice and plenty of fresh lemon slices.

Let's take a look at what ingredients we may need !

Ingridients

  • 5 lemons
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 1 ¼ quarts water

if you have everything prepared let's go step by step to make our vintage lemonade.

Steps

  1. Peel the rinds from the 5 lemons and cut them into 1/2 inch slices. Set the lemons aside.
  2. Place the rinds in a bowl and sprinkle the sugar over them. Let this stand for about one hour, so that the sugar begins to soak up the oils from the lemons.
  3. Bring water to a boil in a covered saucepan and then pour the hot water over the sugared lemon rinds. Allow this mixture to cool for 20 minutes and then remove the rinds.
  4. Squeeze the lemons into another bowl. Pour the juice through a strainer into the sugar mixture. Stir well, pour into pitcher and pop it in the fridge! Serve with ice cubes.

And enjoy the lemonade!!!
