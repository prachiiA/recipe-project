Banana Bread

Description

Looking for a moist banana bread recipe that never fails? Your friends and family will ask for this never-dry banana bread again and again.

The beauty of this banana bread recipe is you don't need a fancy mixer! A mixing bowl, a fork to whisk the eggs, and a sturdy spoon to mix the batter are all you need. The sugar amount is flexible. The original recipe called for a cup of white sugar, but most people do just fine with 3/4 cup, and many are happy with 1/2 cup.

Tip : You can toss in a cup of chopped nuts, raisins, or chocolate chips if you want. Also pick overripe banana for the best result.

let's get started !

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 medium bananas, sliced

If you have got everything ready, let's move forward to actually make it!

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Stir sugar and melted butter together in a large bowl. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter mixture until smooth.

  3. Fold in banana slices, sour cream, and walnuts; transfer into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean, about 1 hour.
  5. Cool loaf in the pan for 10 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack to cool completely.

And enjoy your mist freshly baked Banana Bread!!
